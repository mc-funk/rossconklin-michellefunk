About the Bakken

The Bakken is a museum focused on the historical role of electricity and magnetism in the life sciences and medicine. Founded in 1975, it now resides in a former mansion overlooking lake Mde Maka Ska in south Minneapolis. The museum features a number of fun exhibits, including “Ben Franklin’s Electricity Party” and “Frankensteins Laboratory.” As guests at our wedding you will have access to the Museum Exhibits all day on the 8th. The wedding ceremony and reception will be held upstairs in the Great Hall, with access to the medicinal gardens, and potentially some fun science demonstrations! Make sure you take some time to wander the gardens and enjoy a breathtaking view of the lake.
